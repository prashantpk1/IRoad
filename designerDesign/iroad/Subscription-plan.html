Everything you need to know about our subscription plans

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. For Enterprise plans, we also support custom invoicing and purchase orders.

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on the Professional plan. No credit card is required to start. You can explore all features and decide which plan is right for your business.

When your subscription expires, your account will switch to a read-only mode. You will still be able to access and export your data, but you won't be able to create new records or modify existing ones until you renew your plan.

Each plan has a user limit. If you need more users than your current plan allows, you will need to upgrade to a higher tier. The Enterprise plan offers unlimited users for organizations of any size.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, contact our support team for a full refund.
